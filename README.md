# LukiLinux
The very first major update for LukiLinux is here. The distro is made for absolute performance on old HDD's whilst maintaining a modern and elegant look. Whether you have a low RAM/HDD computer, or even just looking for your new favourite distro, LukiLinux is your new favourite distribution. Based on Linux Mint XFCE, the distro aims to breathe new life into your hardware. There are credits to Linux Mint in the distro whilst completely having a different personality. Try Luki Linux, made by Lukiyan Sakharov today.

About the creator:

The creator, Lukiyan Sakharov is a Russian-South African who loves Linux and everything to do with it.
